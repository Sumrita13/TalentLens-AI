# Job Description: Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team
**Company**: Redrob AI (Series A AI-native talent intelligence platform)
**Location**: Pune/Noida, India (Hybrid — flexible cadence) | Open to relocation candidates from Tier-1 Indian cities
**Employment Type**: Full-time
**Experience Required**: 5–9 years

## Let's be honest about this role
We're a Series A company that just raised our round and we're building a new AI Engineering org from scratch. This is the kind of role where the JD changes every six months because the company changes every six months. If you've spent your career at Google or Meta and you want a well-scoped role with a defined ladder, this isn't it.

## What we need you to do
- **Build from scratch**: You'll be the first few hires in the AI team. You will be building the core matching engine, behavioral scoring models, and LLM-powered justification systems.
- **Production-grade AI**: We don't want research papers; we want production code. You should be comfortable with LLMs (RAG, Fine-tuning), Vector Databases, and high-throughput data processing.
- **Product-minded engineering**: You should care about why a feature is being built, not just how.

## Technical Requirements
- **Deep expertise in Python** and AI frameworks (PyTorch, LangChain, etc.).
- **LLM Experience**: Hands-on experience with RAG pipelines, prompt engineering, and model evaluation.
- **Backend Savvy**: Comfortable with modern backend stacks (FastAPI, Node.js, MongoDB).
- **Scale**: Experience handling high-throughput datasets (100k+ records).

## Cultural Requirements (Redrob Founding Team)
- **High Agency**: You don't wait for instructions.
- **Ship Fast**: You prefer a working prototype over a perfect design.
- **Expert at handling "Messy" data**: Resumes are messy. Our AI needs to be smarter than the chaos.
